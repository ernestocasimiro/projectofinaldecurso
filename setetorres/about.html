Our Story

The Legacy of Excellence

Discover the passion, dedication, and artistry behind one of the world's most celebrated culinary destinations

The Visionary

Chef Miguel Torres

"Cuisine is not about techniques. It's about passion, respect for ingredients, and creating moments of pure pleasure."

Born in Barcelona to a family of culinary artisans, Miguel Torres discovered his passion for gastronomy at an early age, learning the fundamentals of Spanish cuisine from his grandmother.

After training under culinary legends in Paris and Tokyo, Chef Torres developed his distinctive style that harmoniously blends Mediterranean traditions with avant-garde techniques and global influences. His relentless pursuit of perfection and innovative approach to flavor combinations have earned him international acclaim.

In 1995, he established 7TORRES with a singular vision: to create transcendent dining experiences that engage all the senses and evoke profound emotional responses. Under his guidance, the restaurant has evolved into a temple of gastronomy, earning three Michelin stars and a place among the world's culinary elite.

Miguel Torres, Executive Chef
Our Principles

Culinary Philosophy

Ingredient Integrity

We source the finest seasonal ingredients from artisanal producers who share our commitment to excellence. Each component on our menu is selected at its peak, often harvested just hours before service.

Creative Innovation

Our culinary laboratory is where tradition meets innovation. We constantly explore new techniques and flavor combinations while respecting the essence of each ingredient and the foundations of classical cuisine.

Perfect Harmony

Every dish is a carefully orchestrated composition of flavors, textures, aromas, and visual elements. We strive for balance in all aspects, creating multi-sensory experiences that surprise and delight.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

1995

The Beginning

Chef Miguel Torres opens 7TORRES in a modest location with just 24 seats, offering a revolutionary approach to Mediterranean cuisine.

2001

First Michelin Star

After six years of dedication and innovation, 7TORRES receives its first Michelin star, recognizing the restaurant's exceptional cuisine.

2008

Relocation & Expansion

7TORRES moves to its current prestigious location, expanding to accommodate 60 guests and introducing the Chef's Table experience.

2012

Second Michelin Star

The restaurant's continued evolution and commitment to excellence is rewarded with a second Michelin star.

2017

Three Michelin Stars

7TORRES achieves the highest culinary honor, receiving its third Michelin star and joining the elite ranks of the world's finest restaurants.

2023

Global Recognition

Named #7 in the World's 50 Best Restaurants list, cementing its status as a global culinary destination.
